Museum Summary

The Bidwell Gem & Mineral Museum will inspire the next generations of geologists and lapidarists. Families and schools will experience the passion and dedication of Mr. Bidwell and his own private collection of stones and gems.

Goals of the
Bidwell Museum

Preserve

Exhibit the hard work of Larry and the many generations of club members

Share

Be an integral part of the vibrant Glendora community

Educate

Expose the next generation to this beautiful artform and science

"There is a movement within the group to help Larry achieve his museum dream. This is his program."

Anton Mathias, former rock club member

A Different Type of
Geology Museum

Science

Without field work, geophysical techiques, and chemical analysis, lapidary would not be possible

Arts

Cutting, grinding, and polishing stones is beautiful self-expression

Interaction

Educators from all over Southern California have pledged to build impactful exhibits

Exhibit Ideas

We could not honor Larry's memory without mimicking his engaging teaching style. Each exhibit will emerse the budding geologist in a world of science and beauty.
